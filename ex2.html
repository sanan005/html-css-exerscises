Stranger Things

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror drama television series created by the Duffer Brothers and streaming on Netflix. The series is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, and focuses on the investigation of a young boy's disappearance amid supernatural events occurring around the town, including the appearance of a girl with psychokinetic abilities.
