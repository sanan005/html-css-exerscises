Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is simple yet delicious. It combines creamy eggs, savory pancetta, and Parmesan cheese to create a rich and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Method of Preparation

Follow these steps to prepare Spaghetti Carbonara:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat and quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
